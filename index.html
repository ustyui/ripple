RIPPLE

Technology divided us. Technology can reconnect us.

A communication design project exploring how digital isolation traps us in endless observation, and how technology—when designed with intention—can help us break free and reconnect.

Our Vision

Through approachable robots and thoughtful design, RIPPLE makes visible the invisible patterns of our digital lives. From the prison of "likes" and "saves" to the possibility of genuine emotional connection across distances—we explore how awareness can lead to change.

The Problem

Watch how the robot becomes trapped by the accumulation of social media "likes" and "saves"—a metaphor for how digital preparation activities imprison us in endless observation rather than real action.

The Possibility
